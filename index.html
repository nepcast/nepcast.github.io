About & Methodology

Transparent assumptions · Continuous validation

Data Sources

  • DHM stations · river gauges · radar (where available)
  • Satellite: GPM/IMERG, MODIS, Sentinel‑5P
  • Global models: ECMWF, GFS — downscaled

Community sensors (schools/municipalities) planned to fill valley/hill‑slope gaps.

Modeling Approach

  • Numerical: WRF to 1–3 km over complex terrain
  • ML: bias‑correction & post‑processing (quantile mapping, RNN/GBM)
  • Hydro coupling: WRF‑Hydro / VIC for runoff & flood risk

Skill scoring (MAE, CRPS) with spatial k‑fold validation; citizen feedback loop.
